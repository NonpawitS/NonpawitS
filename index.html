02 / Operating System

WAMOER — my problem-solving framework.

A framework I built and now use as a skill to direct my own AI agents. The principle is simple: understand before you execute. It keeps complex, ambiguous work from being solved in the wrong place.

✕ Request → Execute
✓ Request → Why → Assumption → Measure → Options → Execute → Result
W
Why
Understand the real problem, not just the one being asked.
A
Assumption
Surface the unknowns and the hidden assumptions in play.
M
Measure
Find the real data instead of guessing.
O
Options
Compare options with their trade-offs and risks.
E
Execute
Act only after the direction is confirmed.
R
Result
Measure against the success criteria set in Why, and capture the lesson.
When it runs: ambiguous, high-stakes, or multi-step work. When it's skipped: simple, fully-specified tasks that just need doing.
